About Our Project

Teacher's Pet is a piece of software that allows students who are blind/visually impaired to take test/drills. Currently this software only works in the Windows OS, and is heavily out-dated. Our project is to turn Teacher's Pet into a webapp that runs entirely in the user's browser, and works on desktop and mobile platforms.


About Our Sponsor

The American Printing House for the Blind (APH) is an American non-for-profit corporation in Louisville, Kentucky promoting independent living for people who are blind and visually impaired. For over 150 years APH has created unique products and services to support all aspects of daily life without sight.
